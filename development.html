Welcome to Seismism, the earthquake monitor dashboard application.

Seismism by definition is the natural activity or group of phenomena associated with earthquakes. This dashboard application tracks earthquake activity throughout the world and visualizes the data collected through the U.S. Geological Survey data feeds.

Use the data visualization user interface below to explore the earthquake activity.
